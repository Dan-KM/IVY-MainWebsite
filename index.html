About Ivy Community

Ivy Community is a vibrant hub of student developers and designers from universities across Kenya. We bring young talent together to build and ship real products, giving them hands-on experience while still in school and in the early stages of their careers.

Our mission is to help students grow their skills, collaborate on meaningful projects, and transition smoothly into the tech industry—all while creating solutions that make a real impact.
