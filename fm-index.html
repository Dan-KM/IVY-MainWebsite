The Problem: Waste Is Wasted

Kenya faces massive challenges in fresh produce supply chains, leading to waste, inefficiency, and lost opportunities

Supply Chain Waste

Thousands of tons of fresh produce are lost daily due to inefficient distribution networks and lack of proper storage facilities across Kenya's markets.

Farmer Income Loss

Small-scale farmers struggle with price volatility and lack of direct market access, losing up to 40% of potential income due to intermediary markups.

Environmental Impact

Massive organic waste from markets creates environmental problems while missing opportunities for creating value through circular economy principles.

Our Solution: Complete Circular Economy

Fresh Marikiti transforms Kenya's fresh produce ecosystem through innovation, sustainability, and community empowerment

Revolutionizing Fresh Produce Distribution

Fresh Marikiti serves as a vital link between farmers, vendors, and consumers, creating a transparent and efficient supply chain that ensures every delivery meets the highest standards of freshness and quality.
