Welcome to our Immuno App

A Web Application for Hospitals and clinics to Stay on Track with Vaccinations Ensuring Every Child Stays Protected

Our Mission

At IVY Immunization, we believe every child deserves a healthy start in life. Our mission is to simplify vaccination tracking for parents, ensuring no child misses a critical vaccine. By providing an easy-to-use platform, we aim to reduce confusion, eliminate missed appointments, and empower parents with reliable, up-to-date information.

"We solve this with simple, accessible, and reliable technology."

Problem we Address

Many parents in low income areas forget or miss vaccination appointments.

Limited awareness of vaccine schedules beyond clinic visits.

42% of mothers rely on CHWs instead of direct clinic visits, leading to inconsistent vaccination follow-ups.

Missed vaccinations due to poor communication â€” many parents forget or miss appointments without reminders. Missed vaccinations lead to higher risk of preventable diseases.

Hospitals waste vaccine vials because of low turnouts, forcing them to turn away mothers.

Solution Overview

IMMUNO, is a web and SMS/USSD-based platform that helps clinics and hospitals manage vaccination schedules while ensuring parents receive timely reminders.

    Benefits

  • Automated SMS/USSD reminders ensure no child misses a dose.
  • Bulk scheduling and dashboards reduce vaccine wastage and improve clinic efficiency.
  • WhatsApp CHW integration ensures last-mile onboarding and coverage in rural/low-tech areas.
