Smart Vaccination


Welcome to our Immuno App

For Every Community Helping health workers and mothers stay on track with child immunizations through SMS, USSD, and AI-powered tools.

"We solve this with simple, accessible, and reliable technology."

Problem we Address

Missed Vaccinations

Missed vaccinations put children at risk and compromise community health.

Limited Technology Access

Many mothers don't have smartphones or reliable internet connectivity.

Inadequate Tracking Tools

Health workers often lack effective tools to track and follow up with families.

Comprehesive Solution

Our platform serves every stakeholder in the vaccination ecosystem with tailored tools and features.

    For Community Health Workers

  • View child's vaccination records
  • Check appointment schedules
  • Access via USSD (for non-smartphone users)
  • Receive timely SMS reminders

    For Mothers

  • View child's vaccination records
  • Check appointment schedules
  • Access via USSD (for non-smartphone users)
  • Receive timely SMS reminders

    Information Hub

  • Easy-to-read info on vaccine benefits & types
  • Educational content for better health outcomes
  • Multilingual support for diverse communities
  • Offline access to essential information

    Analytics Dashboard

  • Monthly vaccination statistics
  • Missed vs. completed vaccinations tracking
  • Total clients tracked and managed
  • Custom reports for hospitals & NGOs
